PROGRESSIVE WEB APPS (PWA)
2020-06

MDN
https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/Progressive_web_apps
- Technically app needs following 3 to be a PWA 
  1. Secure contexts (HTTPS)
  2. Service Worker(s)
    - Used for caching 
  3. Manifest file


Making PWAs work offline with Service workers
https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/Progressive_web_apps/Offline_Service_workers
- Useful example of how to do service workers + caching 


What are Progressive Web Apps?
https://web.dev/what-are-pwas/
- This article defines PWAs as having the following (doesn't quite sync with Mozilla's definition)
  1. App-like experience and capabilities
  2. Offline capability
  3. Installable 


Readymade "Recipies" for serviceworkers and caching by Mozilla
